- Manning, Raghavan and Schütze, [Introduction to Information Retrieval](https://nlp.stanford.edu/IR-book/).
- Robertson and Zaragoza, [The Probabilistic Relevance Framework: BM25 and Beyond](https://www.nowpublishers.com/article/Details/INR-019), 2009.
- Cormack et al., [Reciprocal Rank Fusion](https://doi.org/10.1145/1571941.1572114), SIGIR 2009.
- Sentence Transformers, [Retrieve & Re-Rank](https://www.sbert.net/examples/sentence_transformer/applications/retrieve_rerank/README.html).
